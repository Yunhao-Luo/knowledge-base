# Artificial Intelligence

## Definition
**Artificial Intelligence (AI)** refers to computational systems capable of performing tasks that typically require human intelligence, including learning, reasoning, perception, and decision-making [[xr-ai-intersection]](../analyses/xr-ai-intersection.md).

## Key AI Technologies in XR Context
- **Machine Learning**: Pattern recognition and prediction models
- **Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs)**: Semantic understanding of objects and contexts [[augmented-object-intelligence]](../concepts/augmented-object-intelligence.md)
- **Computer Vision**: Object detection, tracking, and scene understanding
- **Generative Models**: Content creation using techniques like GANs

## AI in Extended Reality
AI is increasingly integrated with XR technologies to:
- Create realistic virtual environments and avatars [[xr-ai-topics]](../analyses/xr-ai-topics.md)
- Understand user behavior and preferences
- Enable intelligent interaction with physical objects (AOI)
- Support human-AI collaboration in mixed reality

## Research Gaps
Current AI-XR research shows:
- Limited generative model application for content creation
- Underrepresentation of robustness testing across populations
- Minimal discussion of ethical implications [[xr-ai-intersection]](../analyses/xr-ai-intersection.md)
